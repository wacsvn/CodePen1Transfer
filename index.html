Lingardinho

English footballer Jesse Lingard is very often compared by fans to the Brazilian legend Ronaldinho.

Jesse Lingard plays for Manchester United as an attacking midfielder, and is regarded by most to have not the greatest stats, especially after falling out of favor of manager Ole Gunnar Solskjaer. Yet, a select group of fans on the internet have begun to refer to him as Lingardinho (a combination of his name and Brazilian legend Ronaldinho), an initial joke that has gained some credence following his success while on loan to West Ham United.
